Documentation

Home Page - Our Home Page displays our main bar graph of the total budget for education by category from Prince George's County. The total county amount of budget for education is displayed directly below the graph.

The Home Page also includes a selectable list of all payees filtered from the API. When a payee is selected, a pie chart will display the their allocated budget by category, as well as their total budget allocation and it's percentage of the overall education budget. Hovering the mouse cursor over a section of the pie chart will display the section's category name and amount, and clicking categories from the legend makes them pop out for easier visibility/accessibility.
